How It Works

Three simple steps to save food and feed communities

📦

1. Donate

Restaurants, caterers, or households list surplus food with quantity, location, and pickup window.

📍

2. Match

Our system instantly notifies nearby NGOs and displays food on a live GPS-powered map.

🚚

3. Deliver

NGOs claim the food, pick it up, and mark it as delivered — all tracked in real time.
